About

A bit about myself.

I am from west Phillidelphia, where I was born and raised.. whoops, that was the wrong about page. My name is Vann Vaughan, and I am orignially from a small, one horse town called Cartersville in Georgia. My mom moved us to Dallas in 1998 for a career opportunity and we have never looked back, well maybe once. I went to all of the great schools that the city of Plano, where we lived, had to offer. After graduating high school, I attended Collin College for a few semesters and realized I just wanted to work.

I started out working in the construction industry where I spent most of my professional career, excluding my 2 year sabbatical managing a local gun dealer and bouncing through various odd jobs. Eventually I landed at my previous employer, Aurora Roof Systems, where I realized construction just wasn't my cup of tea anymore. Here is where I had the opportunity to re-build their webpage and their logo. Finally, I found my passion, web design.
